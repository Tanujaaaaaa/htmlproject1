Atlantis

The Fabled City Beneath the Waves

According to ancient Greek philosopher Plato, Atlantis was an advanced civilization that disappeared underwater around 9600 BCE. Its location remains a mystery, with theories suggesting it may have been in the Atlantic Ocean.

  • Legend: Atlantis is perhaps the most famous lost city, originally described by the ancient Greek philosopher Plato. According to Plato, Atlantis was a powerful and technologically advanced civilization that sank into the ocean after its people became corrupt.

  • Location: Though its exact location remains unknown, various theories suggest it might have been in the Mediterranean, the Atlantic Ocean, or even Antarctica.

  • Significance: Atlantis has become a symbol of a lost golden age and is often depicted in popular culture as a mysterious underwater city.
